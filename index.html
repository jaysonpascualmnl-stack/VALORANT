Valorant is a free-to-play 5v5 tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games for PC, featuring character-based gameplay where players select "Agents" with unique abilities. The main game mode involves teams attacking or defending by planting or defusing a bomb called the "Spike".


The Essentials The Goal: Attackers try to plant a bomb (the Spike); Defenders try to stop them. First to 13 rounds wins. The Combat: Gunplay is lethal and precise. One headshot usually equals one kill. The Twist: You play as Agents with unique abilities (smokes, heals, flashes, or drones) that help you control the map. The Economy: You manage "Credits" to buy better guns and abilities as the match progresses. Why It’s Popular Roles: Whether you like being the aggressive "fragger" (Duelist) or the strategic "brains" (Controller), there’s a spot for you. Style: It features a vibrant, near-future aesthetic with a deep story involving a "Mirror Earth" conflict. Accessibility: It’s free-to-play and runs smoothly even on older PCs.
